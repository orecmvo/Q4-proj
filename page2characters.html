NOLI ME TANGERE

Important Characters
CHARACTER DESCRIPTION

SISA
  • She is the mother of Basilio and Crispin, and the wife of Pedro.

  • She is very caring, however she is also submissive. Her husband was giving her nothing
    but stress, but it seemed like it was nothing for her.

  • Her role is very significant as she was used by Rizal as a representation of the women back in Rizal’s time.

BASILIO
  • Sisa’s oldest child

  • He is a child who’s strong despite all of the hardships that surround him.

  • At a young age, he has a very strong mindset and has a sense of maturity.

  • He was used by Rizal as a representation of the youth during the time of the Spaniards,
    where child abuse and child labour was present.

CRISPIN
  • Sisa’s youngest child and is a Sakristan

  • Unlike Basilio, he is a child who’s weak to face all hardships that surround him.

  • Similar to Basilio, he was used by Rizal as a representation of the youth during the time
    of the Spaniards, where child abuse and child labour was present.
